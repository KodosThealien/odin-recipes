Let's make Kodos famous lentil soup!

Description

This is my recipe for lentil soup. It's absolute delicious, vegetarian (like me) and packed with protein, fiber, Vitamin A and Iron.

It's made from red lentils to give it a nice color but you could easily substitute black or yellow. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Med Onion - peeled and diced
  • 2 Med Carrots - peeled and diced
  • 4 Garlic Cloves - minced
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1/4 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 10 oz. Red Lentils - rinsed
  • 7 Cups Vegetable Broth
  • Salt - to taste
