Let's make Kodos famous lentil soup!

Description

This is my recipe for lentil soup. It's absolutely delicious, vegetarian (like me) and packed with Protein, Fiber, Vitamin A and Iron.

It's made from red lentils to give it a nice red/orange color but you could easily substitute black or yellow if you'd like. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Med Onion - peeled and diced
  • 2 Med Carrots - peeled and diced
  • 4 Garlic Cloves - minced
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1/4 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 10 oz. Red Lentils - rinsed
  • 7 Cups Vegetable Broth
  • Salt - to taste

Steps

  1. In Large Soup pot, saute Carrots and Onion in Olive Oil for about 4 mins
  2. Add Garlic and Spices and cook for another minute
  3. Add Tomato Paste, Rinsed Lentils, and Vegetable Broth. Bring the soup to a boil, reduce the heat to low and simmer for 25 - 30 minutes
  4. Remove the pot from the heat and puree with an immersion blender until smooth
  5. Serve hot. Feel free to squeeze a little lemon juice or sprinkle some fresh herbs on top.
  6. Enjoy!!!!
