Veggie Fried Rice Recipe!

Description

Glorious and flavorful. This is a fresh healthy take on a carry out classic. Less greasy and much tastier than your local takeaway. We use fresh vegetables in this recipe that aren't just delicious but also a feast for the eyes.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tsp + 2 Tbsp Avocado or Safflower Oil
  • 2 Eggs - whisked together
  • 1 Sm White Onion - finely chopped
  • 2 Med Carrots - finely chopped
  • 2 Cups additional Veggies - Snow Peas, Broccoli, Cabbage, Bell Pepper. Go CRAZY!!!
  • 1/4 tsp Salt - to taste
  • 1 Tbsp Fresh Ginger - grated or finely minced
  • 2 Lg Garlic Cloves - pressed or minced
  • Pinch Red Pepper Flakes
  • 2 Cups Cooked Brown Rice
  • 1 Cup Greens - Spinach, Kale etc
  • 3 Green Onions - chopped
  • 1 Tbsp Tamari or Soy Sauce - Reduced sodium!!!
  • 1 tsp Toasted Sesame Oil
  • Chili Garlic Sauce or Sriracha - for serving

Steps

  1. Make sure all ingredients are prepped and within easy reach!!! This recipe comes together quickly!
  2. Warm Large Cast Iron or Stainless skillet over Med-high heat until a few drops of water evaporate in a couple seconds. Add 1 1/2 tsp oil and swirl the pan to coat bottom. Add scrambled eggs and swirl to cover bottom. Cook until lightly set. Transfer eggs to a bowl and wipe pan with heat proof spatula
  3. Return pan to heat and add 1 Tbsp oil. Add Onions and Carrots and cook, stirring often until onions are translucent and carrots are tender. About 3 - 4 minutes
  4. Add remaining veggies and salt. Continue cooking stirring occasionally until veggies are cooked through and tuen golden on the edges. About 3 - 5 more minutes. Break the scrambled eggs into small pieces
  5. Use a big spatula or spoon and transfer the contents of the pan to the bowl with the eggs. Return the pan to heat and add remaining 1 Tbsp oil. Add Ginger, Garlic and Red Pepper Flakes and cook until fragrant, stirring constantly. About 30 seconds. Add Rice and mix. Cook, stirring occasionally until the Rice is hot and turning golden at the edges. About 3 - 5 minutes.
  6. Add Greens and Green Onions and stir to combine. Remove the pan from heart and stir in Tamari and Sesame Oil. Take a taste and and adjust Tamari to your liking.
  7. Divide into bowls and serve immediately. Serve with Chili Garlic or Sriracha on the side.
  8. Enjoy!
